Spacious Family Room for Your Comfort

Experience the ultimate family getaway in our spacious Family Room at the Aswan Hotel. Designed with families in mind, our Family Room offers ample space and essential amenities for a memorable stay.

Room Features:

  • Family-Friendly Accommodation: Enjoy spacious and comfortable accommodation suitable for families, featuring multiple beds and a cozy seating area.
  • Kitchenette: Prepare snacks and meals for your family in the convenience of your own kitchenette, equipped with a microwave, refrigerator, and coffee maker.
  • In-room Entertainment: Keep the whole family entertained with a flat-screen TV, DVD player, and a selection of family-friendly movies.
  • Play Area: Let the kids play and have fun in our designated play area, complete with toys and games to keep them entertained.
  • Private Bathroom: Freshen up in your private bathroom stocked with essential toiletries, towels, and a bathtub for a relaxing soak.
