Experience Comfort and Style in Our Double Room

Welcome to the Aswan Hotel, where comfort meets style in our Double Room. Perfect for couples or those seeking a bit more space, our Double Room offers a cozy retreat with modern amenities.

Room Features:

  • Spacious Accommodation: Relax in our spacious Double Room furnished with a comfortable queen-size bed and elegant decor.
  • Modern Amenities: Enjoy complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi, a flat-screen TV, and a work desk, ensuring a comfortable stay.
  • In-room Dining: Indulge in our in-room dining service, offering a variety of delicious meals and beverages.
  • Private Bathroom: Refresh in your private bathroom equipped with a shower, premium toiletries, and fluffy towels.
  • City Views: Admire stunning city views from your room's large windows, providing a glimpse into the vibrant energy of Aswan.
