Experience Tranquility and Comfort in Our Single Room

Welcome to the Aswan Hotel, where your comfort and relaxation are our top priority. Our elegantly designed Single Room offers a perfect sanctuary for solo travelers seeking both convenience and luxury in the heart of Aswan.

Room Features:

  • Cozy and Comfortable: Unwind in a plush single bed adorned with high-quality linens and pillows, ensuring a restful night's sleep after a day of exploring the wonders of Aswan.
  • Modern Amenities: Stay connected with complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, and a well-equipped work desk, perfect for business travelers.
  • Thoughtful Details: Enjoy the convenience of a mini-bar, in-room safe, and a coffee/tea maker to start your day right. The room also features individually controlled air conditioning to keep you comfortable year-round.
  • En-suite Bathroom: Refresh and rejuvenate in the pristine en-suite bathroom, complete with a spacious shower, premium toiletries, fluffy towels, and a hairdryer.
  • Stunning Views: Delight in the serene views of the Nile River or the vibrant cityscape of Aswan from your room's large windows, adding a touch of magic to your stay.
